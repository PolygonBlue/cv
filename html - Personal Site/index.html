Deep in the recesses of the "Kenny's-End" Cave system, I found the latest in a bizarre string of clues. I awoke at 0430 this morning in a vain attempt to reach the maw by early 0700, before the sun's heat made things too unbearable to travel across the barren Mojave Desert..

For me, this all started in late 2018 with none other than an out of place comment on a post I made on reddit. The comment was in response to a post I made regarding a bird I was watching. The comment simply read as "Hay [My Name], it's been a while. we went to high school together way back when. We should catch up; here's my cell [...]." Though I'm not exactly the most private individual, I was taken aback that the account knew my name (something I never shared on my reddit account). After some diliberation, my curriosity got the better of me. I called the number. What followed was simultaniously intruiging and off-putting. The phone rang twice before playing a strange pitch-key like 8-bit tone (think classic games like mario or space invaders) followed by a string of numbers. I hastily scrawed them on an old receipt I hadn't tossed from my earlier take out. The numbers read "1.5.6.6.7.1.3.8.1." I was obviously a bit confussed and didn't know what to make of these. After askign around, I found a guy who somehow was able to extrapulate coordinates out of these seemingly random numbers.

The First Clue

To say I was a little intruiged would be an understatment. That location wasn't too far way and I had some off time built up. The following weekend, I recruited my brother to tag along and we traveled the near three hour trek to this location. At first, it looked like no more than an empty landscape. Before long I decided to go to the exact location the coordinates pointed to. Luckily I had a spade in my trunk to dig. About 3ft in the dry dirt was a small time capule of sorts. Therein was a small collection of phones and an assortment of envelopes. Was this some sort of Geo-cashe? I'd never done one before, but knew of them nonetheless. I wasn't keen on just picking up a random phone from a random place. Who knows wth is on that thing. The FBI might be looking for it for all I know. I the end, I picked up a phone and turned it on.
